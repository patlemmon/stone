Void Width Visualization Options

5 approaches to help players gauge gap widths when selecting stones

Option 1 — Width Labels on Voids

Small measurements appear in each open gap along the wall surface during card selection. Like a tape measure overlay showing exact inches.

During card selection phase

Option 2 — Color-Coded Gap Highlighting

Open voids are highlighted with colors indicating fit quality against cards in hand. Green = a card fits perfectly, yellow = close, gray = nothing fits.

Gaps colored by fit quality

Option 3 — Stone Silhouette Preview

When hovering a card, translucent silhouettes appear at every valid placement on the wall. Player instantly sees "this stone fits here and here."

Hovering the 8" wide stone card

Option 4 — Gap Ruler Overlay

A subtle ruler/measuring bar drawn along the top build surface with tick marks and width labels between filled stones. Always visible during play.

Persistent ruler along build surface

Option 5 — Card Fit Indicators

Each hand card shows a badge indicating how many valid placements exist. Helps players avoid picking stones that won't fit anywhere.

Badges on hand cards
